Extension for Japanese text parsing and sentence mining, fully integrated with JPDB. Supports parsing and mining from subtitles (asbplayer), web apps like Readwok and TTSU Reader, and many other sites. Automatically detects Japanese text on supported pages, provides context-aware parsing, and allows you to send sentences and vocabulary directly to JPDB for efficient language learning. Includes customizable keyboard shortcuts, advanced filtering, and support for both manual and automatic parsing. Designed for learners who want to streamline their Japanese study workflow across a wide range of online resources.
